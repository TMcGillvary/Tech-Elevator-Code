Pusheen Fan Page

Pusheen is a cartoon cat who is the subject of comic strips and sticker sets on Facebook, Instagram, iMessage, and other social media platforms. Pusheen was created in 2010 by Claire Belton and Andrew Duff for a comic strip on their website, Everyday Cute.
